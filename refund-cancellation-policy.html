Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear and fair policies for your peace of mind

Cancellation Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while protecting our ability to serve other guests.

Cancellation Timeline

48+ hours before check-in

Full Refund - 100% of booking amount refunded

24-48 hours before check-in

50% Refund - 50% of booking amount refunded

Less than 24 hours before check-in

No Refund - Booking amount is non-refundable

No-Show

No Refund - Full amount charged

How to Cancel

  • Email us at booking@puribeach-resort.com
  • Call us at +91 8059815571
  • WhatsApp us at +91 8059815571
  • Provide your booking reference number

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds are made to the original payment method
  • Processing fees may apply for certain payment methods
  • Bank transfer refunds may take 3-5 business days to reflect

Special Circumstances

In certain situations, we may offer exceptions to our standard cancellation policy:

  • Medical Emergency: Full refund with valid medical documentation
  • Natural Disaster: Full refund or reschedule option
  • Government Travel Restrictions: Full refund with official documentation
  • Death in Family: Full refund with death certificate

Booking Modifications

Date changes are subject to availability and rate differences.

  • Date changes made 7+ days before check-in: No additional charges
  • Date changes made 3-7 days before check-in: 10% modification fee
  • Date changes made less than 3 days before check-in: 25% modification fee

No-Show Policy

If you fail to check-in on the scheduled date without prior notice:

  • Full amount will be charged
  • No refund will be issued
  • Room may be released to other guests

Force Majeure

We are not responsible for cancellations due to circumstances beyond our control:

  • Natural disasters, extreme weather events
  • Government actions, restrictions, or regulations
  • Terrorist activities, civil unrest
  • Pandemics, public health emergencies
  • Transportation strikes or disruptions
  • Utility failures, power outages

In such cases, we will offer rescheduling options or full refunds.

Important Notes

  • All cancellations must be confirmed in writing
  • Refund amounts exclude any processing fees or third-party charges
  • Group bookings (5+ rooms) may have different cancellation terms
  • Seasonal rates and special packages may have non-refundable deposits

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this cancellation and refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this website with an updated effective date.
