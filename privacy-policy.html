Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when:

  • Making a booking inquiry through our website
  • Contacting us via phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • Browsing our website (technical data only)

Types of Information

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Booking Information: Check-in/out dates, room preferences, special requests
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your bookings
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating our business
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure payment processing for bookings
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Know what personal information we hold about you

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage
  • Provide personalized content and experiences

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
