How to Reach Puri by Train, Road, and Air

Published: April 21, 2025 | 8 min read | By Puri Beach Resort Team

Puri, one of India's most sacred and beautiful coastal cities, is well-connected by rail, road, and air. Whether you're traveling from nearby Bhubaneswar, distant Kolkata, or any other part of India, reaching Puri is convenient and straightforward. This comprehensive guide covers all transportation options to help you plan your journey to Puri Beach Resort with ease.

Pro Tip: Puri Beach Resort is located on Marine Drive Road, easily accessible from all major transportation hubs in Puri. We can arrange airport/railway station transfers for our guests.

Reaching Puri by Train

Most Popular and Convenient Option

Traveling by train is the most popular way to reach Puri, thanks to excellent rail connectivity and affordable fares.

Puri Railway Station

Puri has its own railway station (PURI), which is a major terminus on the East Coast Railway. The station is well-maintained and offers various amenities including waiting rooms, retiring rooms, and food stalls.

Major Trains to Puri

  • Purushottam Express: New Delhi to Puri (28 hours)
  • Nandan Kanan Express: New Delhi to Puri (28 hours)
  • Konark Express: Hyderabad to Puri (21 hours)
  • Howrah-Puri Express: Kolkata to Puri (7 hours)
  • Dhauli Express: Kolkata to Puri (7 hours)
  • Sri Jagannath Express: Chennai to Puri (24 hours)
  • Puri Express: Visakhapatnam to Puri (14 hours)

From Major Cities

  • Kolkata (Howrah): 7-8 hours, multiple daily trains
  • Bhubaneswar: 1.5-2 hours, frequent local trains
  • Chennai: 24-26 hours, direct trains available
  • Hyderabad: 20-22 hours, direct trains available
  • New Delhi: 27-30 hours, daily trains available
  • Mumbai: 32-36 hours, requires connection

Getting to Puri Beach Resort from Railway Station

  • Auto-rickshaw: 10-15 minutes, ₹30-50
  • Cycle rickshaw: 15-20 minutes, ₹20-30
  • Taxi: 10 minutes, ₹50-100
  • Hotel Pickup: Contact Puri Beach Resort for arranged transfer

Reaching Puri by Road

Scenic and Flexible Travel

Traveling by road offers flexibility and the opportunity to enjoy scenic routes. Puri is well-connected by national highways and state roads.

From Bhubaneswar (Nearest Major City)

  • Distance: 60 km
  • Travel Time: 1.5-2 hours
  • Route: NH-16 (Bhubaneswar-Konark-Puri Marine Drive)
  • Transport Options: Private taxi, bus, self-drive

From Kolkata

  • Distance: 500 km
  • Travel Time: 8-10 hours
  • Route: NH-16 via Kharagpur, Balasore, Cuttack
  • Transport Options: Private taxi, state-run buses, private buses

From Other Cities

  • Chennai: 1,200 km, 20-22 hours by road
  • Hyderabad: 1,100 km, 18-20 hours by road
  • Visakhapatnam: 450 km, 8-9 hours by road
  • Ranchi: 500 km, 9-10 hours by road

Bus Services to Puri

  • OSRTC (Odisha State Road Transport): Regular services from all major Odisha cities
  • Private Operators: Volvo and luxury buses from Kolkata, Bhubaneswar, Cuttack
  • Bus Stand Location: Puri Bus Stand, 1.5 km from city center

Self-Drive Option

  • Road Condition: Generally good, especially NH-16
  • Fuel Stations: Available at regular intervals
  • Parking: Free parking available at Puri Beach Resort
  • Best Route: Bhubaneswar-Konark Marine Drive (scenic coastal road)

Reaching Puri by Air

Fastest but Requires Connection

Puri does not have its own airport. The nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport in Bhubaneswar, located 60 km away.

Biju Patnaik International Airport (BBI)

  • Location: Bhubaneswar, Odisha
  • Distance from Puri: 60 km
  • Airlines: Air India, IndiGo, SpiceJet, GoAir, Vistara
  • Major Connections: Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Hyderabad, Bangalore

Flight Duration from Major Cities

  • Delhi to Bhubaneswar: 2-2.5 hours
  • Mumbai to Bhubaneswar: 2.5-3 hours
  • Kolkata to Bhubaneswar: 1-1.5 hours
  • Chennai to Bhubaneswar: 2 hours
  • Bangalore to Bhubaneswar: 2-2.5 hours
  • Hyderabad to Bhubaneswar: 1.5-2 hours

From Airport to Puri

  • Pre-paid Taxi: ₹1,200-1,500, 1.5-2 hours
  • Private Taxi: ₹1,500-2,000, 1.5-2 hours
  • Bus: OSRTC airport shuttle to Puri, ₹200-300, 2-2.5 hours
  • Hotel Transfer: Puri Beach Resort can arrange airport pickup

Recommended Travel Routes

Route 1: Kolkata to Puri (Most Popular)

Option A: Train

Take Howrah-Puri Express or Dhauli Express from Howrah Station. Journey time: 7-8 hours. Most convenient and time-efficient.

Option B: Flight + Road

Fly Kolkata to Bhubaneswar (1 hour), then taxi to Puri (1.5 hours). Total time: 3-4 hours. Most expensive but fastest.

Option C: Road

Drive via NH-16. Journey time: 8-10 hours. Good for those who enjoy road trips and want flexibility.

Route 2: Delhi to Puri

Option A: Train

Take Purushottam Express or Nandan Kanan Express from New Delhi. Journey time: 27-30 hours. Most economical.

Option B: Flight + Road

Fly Delhi to Bhubaneswar (2 hours), then taxi to Puri (1.5 hours). Total time: 4-5 hours. Fastest option.

Route 3: South India to Puri

Option A: Train

Direct trains available from Chennai and Hyderabad. Journey time: 20-24 hours. Economical but long.

Option B: Flight + Road

Fly to Bhubaneswar from Chennai/Hyderabad (2 hours), then taxi to Puri (1.5 hours). Total time: 4-5 hours. Recommended.

Local Transportation in Puri

Getting Around the City

  • Auto-rickshaws: Most convenient for short distances. Metered or negotiated fares.
  • Cycle rickshaws: Eco-friendly and budget-friendly. Good for short distances.
  • Taxis: Available for day trips to Konark, Chilika Lake, and nearby attractions.
  • Rental Scooters: Available for independent exploration. Requires valid license.
  • Hotel Transport: Puri Beach Resort can arrange transportation for guests.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Travel

  • October to March: Pleasant weather, ideal for travel
  • Avoid Monsoon: July-September can cause travel disruptions due to rain
  • Book in Advance: Especially during peak season (October-February) and festivals

Packing Tips

  • Light cotton clothes for summer, light woolens for winter
  • Comfortable walking shoes for temple and beach visits
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and hat for sun protection
  • Rain gear if traveling during monsoon
  • Traditional attire for temple visits
  • Essential medications and first aid kit

Booking Tips

  • Train Tickets: Book via IRCTC or authorized agents. Tatkal booking available 1 day before.
  • Flight Tickets: Book in advance for better rates, especially during peak season.
  • Bus Tickets: Book online via OSRTC or private operator websites.
  • Accommodation: Book Puri Beach Resort in advance, especially during festivals.

Distance from Nearby Attractions

  • Bhubaneswar: 60 km (1.5-2 hours drive)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does Puri have an airport?

A: No, Puri does not have its own airport. The nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport in Bhubaneswar, 60 km away.

Q: How far is Puri from Bhubaneswar?

A: Puri is 60 km from Bhubaneswar, approximately 1.5-2 hours by road or 1.5-2 hours by train.

Q: What is the best way to reach Puri from Kolkata?

A: Train is the most popular option (7-8 hours). Flight to Bhubaneswar + taxi is fastest (4-5 hours). Road trip is good for flexibility (8-10 hours).

Q: Are there direct trains from Delhi to Puri?

A: Yes, direct trains like Purushottam Express and Nandan Kanan Express connect Delhi to Puri, taking 27-30 hours.

Q: How can I reach Puri from South India?

A: Fly to Bhubaneswar from Chennai/Hyderabad (2 hours), then take a taxi to Puri (1.5 hours). Direct trains are also available but take 20-24 hours.

Q: Is it safe to drive to Puri?

A: Yes, road conditions are generally good, especially on NH-16. However, exercise caution during monsoon and night driving.

Q: Can Puri Beach Resort arrange airport pickup?

A: Yes, we can arrange airport/railway station transfers for our guests. Contact us in advance to book this service.
