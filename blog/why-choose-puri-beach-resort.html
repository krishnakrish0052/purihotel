Why Choose Puri Beach Resort for Your Stay

Published: April 21, 2025 | 5 min read | By Puri Beach Resort Team

When planning your trip to Puri, choosing the right accommodation can make or break your experience. Puri Beach Resort stands out as the premier choice for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and luxury on the beautiful coast of Odisha. Here's why discerning travelers choose us for their Puri getaway.

Prime Location: Located on Marine Drive Road, near Light House Swargadwar, we offer the perfect blend of beachfront luxury and convenient access to Puri's main attractions.

1. Unbeatable Beachfront Location

Right on Swargadwar Beach

Puri Beach Resort is situated directly on the famous Swargadwar Beach, giving our guests immediate access to the golden sands and stunning Bay of Bengal views. Wake up to the sound of waves, enjoy breathtaking sunrises from your room, and take leisurely walks on the beach whenever you desire. Our Marine Drive location also means you're just steps away from the beach market and local attractions.

2. Close Proximity to Jagannath Temple

Only 2-3 km from Sacred Temple

For pilgrims and spiritual seekers, our location is ideal. The magnificent Jagannath Temple, one of India's Char Dham pilgrimage sites, is just 2-3 km from our resort. This means you can easily visit the temple for morning prayers or participate in evening aartis without spending hours in transit. We're perfectly positioned for those who want both spiritual experiences and beach relaxation.

3. Luxury AC Rooms with Sea Views

Comfortable & Well-Appointed Rooms

Our well-appointed AC rooms are designed for ultimate comfort. Many of our rooms offer stunning sea views, allowing you to enjoy the beauty of Puri's coastline from the comfort of your room. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including comfortable bedding, air conditioning, television, and attached bathrooms. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, we have the perfect room for your needs.

4. Refreshing Swimming Pool

Relaxation & Recreation

Take a dip in our sparkling swimming pool, the perfect way to cool off after a day of sightseeing or temple visits. Our pool area is well-maintained and offers a serene environment for relaxation. Whether you want to swim laps, lounge by the poolside, or simply enjoy the refreshing water, our pool adds an extra layer of luxury to your stay.

5. Multi-Cuisine Restaurant

Delicious Dining Experience

Our in-house multi-cuisine restaurant serves a delightful array of dishes to satisfy every palate. From authentic Odisha cuisine to popular Indian and international dishes, our chefs prepare each meal with care and expertise. Enjoy fresh seafood, traditional dalma, and other local specialties, or opt for familiar comfort food. Our restaurant offers both indoor and outdoor seating options.

6. 24/7 Room Service

Always at Your Service

We understand that comfort knows no time constraints. Our 24/7 room service ensures that you can enjoy delicious meals, snacks, or beverages whenever you desire. Whether you're craving a midnight snack or an early morning breakfast, our attentive staff is always ready to serve you with a smile.

7. Free WiFi Connectivity

Stay Connected

Stay connected with family and friends or catch up on work with our complimentary high-speed WiFi available throughout the resort. Share your Puri experiences on social media, stream your favorite content, or simply stay in touch with the outside world while enjoying your beach vacation.

8. Doctor on Call

Your Health & Safety

Your well-being is our priority. We provide doctor-on-call services to ensure that medical assistance is available whenever needed. Whether it's a minor health concern or emergency, our team can quickly arrange for medical attention, giving you peace of mind during your stay.

9. Travel Desk Assistance

Explore Puri with Ease

Our travel desk is here to help you make the most of your Puri visit. Whether you want to arrange a day trip to Konark Sun Temple, explore Chilika Lake, or book local transportation, our knowledgeable staff can assist you with all your travel needs. We can also provide recommendations for local attractions and help you plan your itinerary.

10. Free Parking Facility

Hassle-Free Parking

For guests traveling by car, we offer free parking facilities. Our secure parking area can accommodate both cars and two-wheelers, ensuring that your vehicle is safe while you enjoy your stay. No need to worry about finding parking or paying extra fees – we've got you covered.

11. Marriage Mandap & Event Facilities

Perfect for Celebrations

Planning a wedding or special event? Our marriage mandap and event facilities make Puri Beach Resort an ideal venue for celebrations. With our beautiful beachfront location, excellent catering services, and experienced event coordination team, we can help make your special day truly memorable.

12. World-Class Hospitality

Warm & Welcoming Service

What truly sets Puri Beach Resort apart is our commitment to exceptional hospitality. Our dedicated staff goes above and beyond to ensure that every guest feels welcomed and cared for. From the moment you arrive until your departure, we strive to make your stay comfortable, memorable, and exceeding your expectations.
