About Our Resort

Your Gateway to Serenity

Puri Beach Resort, located on Marine Drive Road near Light House Swargadwar, is one of the best hotels in Puri offering luxury beachfront accommodation. Just 2-3 km from the famous Jagannath Temple and right on the beautiful Swargadwar Beach, our resort provides the perfect blend of comfort and convenience for pilgrims and tourists.

As a premier beach resort in Odisha, we offer stunning sea views, premium AC rooms, a swimming pool, multi-cuisine restaurant, and world-class hospitality. Whether you're visiting for the Rath Yatra or a peaceful beach vacation, Puri Beach Resort is your ideal destination for an unforgettable stay in Puri.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out timings?

Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability.

How far is Puri Beach Resort from Jagannath Temple?

Our resort is located on Marine Drive Road, just 2-3 km from the famous Jagannath Temple, making it convenient for pilgrims and tourists.

Do you provide parking facilities?

Yes, we offer free parking for our guests. Our secure parking area can accommodate cars and two-wheelers.

Is the hotel beach-facing?

Yes, Puri Beach Resort is located right on the beautiful Swargadwar Beach, offering stunning sea views from many of our rooms.

What amenities are available at the resort?

We offer AC rooms, swimming pool, multi-cuisine restaurant, 24/7 room service, free WiFi, doctor on call, travel desk, and marriage mandap facilities.

How can I book a room at Puri Beach Resort?

You can book by calling us at +91 8059815571 or messaging us on WhatsApp. Our team will assist you with availability and booking.

Is the hotel suitable for families?

Absolutely! We have family-friendly rooms and suites, a swimming pool, and are located near the safe Swargadwar Beach, perfect for family vacations.

Do you accept credit cards?

Yes, we accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and digital payment methods for your convenience.
